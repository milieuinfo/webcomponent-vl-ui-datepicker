vl-datepicker demo

Meer voorbeelden en documentatie.

Datepicker

            
                <vl-datepicker></vl-datepicker>

Datepicker met custom format

            
                <vl-datepicker format="d/m/Y"></vl-datepicker>

Datepicker met vandaag als vooringevulde datum

            
                <vl-datepicker format="d/m/Y" selected-date='today'></vl-datepicker>

Datepicker met min/max datums

            
                <vl-datepicker min-date="10-05-2019" max-date="15-05-2019"></vl-datepicker>

Date-rangepicker

            
                <vl-datepicker type="range"></vl-datepicker>

Date-rangepicker met niet-beschikbare datums

            
                <vl-datepicker format="Y-m-d" type="range" min-date="2019-04-10" max-date="2019-06-15" disabled-dates='["2019-04-15","2019-04-17","2019-04-19",{"from": "2019-04-25", "to": "2019-05-06"}]'></vl-datepicker>

Datepicker met alternatieve visualisatie

            
                <vl-datepicker type="date" visual-format="l d M Y"></vl-datepicker>

Timepicker

            
                <vl-datepicker type="time" format="H:i"></vl-datepicker>

Timepicker met min/max (9:00-17:00)

            
                <vl-datepicker type="time" format="H:i" min-time="09:00" max-time="17:00"></vl-datepicker>

Timepicker met AM/PM notatie

            
                <vl-datepicker type="time" format="H:i" am-pm></vl-datepicker>

Date en timepicker

            
                <vl-datepicker type="date-time" format="d-m-Y H:i" am-pm></vl-datepicker>

Datepicker kan programmatisch gewijzigd worden

            
                <vl-datepicker id="datepicker-1"></vl-datepicker>
                <button is="vl-button" onclick="document.querySelector('#datepicker-1').value = '01.12.2019'">Kies 01.12.2019</button>
                
                <vl-datepicker id="datepicker-2" format="d/m/Y"></vl-datepicker>
                <button is="vl-button" onclick="document.querySelector('#datepicker-2').value = '01/12/2019'">Kies 01/12/2019</button>
                
                <vl-datepicker id="datepicker-3" type="range"></vl-datepicker>
                <button is="vl-button" onclick="document.querySelector('#datepicker-3').value = ['01.12.2019', '10.12.2019']">Kies 01.12.2019 tot 10.12.2019</button>
                
                <vl-datepicker id="datepicker-4" type="time" format="H:i"></vl-datepicker>
                <button is="vl-button" onclick="document.querySelector('#datepicker-4').value = ['11:55']">Kies 11:55</button>
                
                <vl-datepicker id="datepicker-5" type="date-time" format="d-m-Y H:i" am-pm></vl-datepicker>
                <button is="vl-button" onclick="document.querySelector('#datepicker-5').value = ['01-12-2019 11:55']">Kies 01-12-2019 11:55</button>

Datepicker error

            
                <vl-datepicker error></vl-datepicker>

Datepicker succes

            
                <vl-datepicker success></vl-datepicker>

Modal met datepicker

            
                <button is="vl-button" data-vl-modal-open="modal-1">Open</button>
                <vl-modal id="modal-1" data-title="Modal" closable not-cancellable>
                    <span slot="content">
                        Datepicker in een modal
                        <vl-datepicker></vl-datepicker>
                    </span>
                </vl-modal>
